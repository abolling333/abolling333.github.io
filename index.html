Virginia Energy Rebrand

Formerly the Dept. of Mines, Minerals, and Energy, this massive project involved organizing and collaborating with department directors, application developers, UX and web design teams, content creators, marketers, other state agencies, and third-party suppliers. The project had a non-negotiable deadline and had to adhere to strict regulations imparted by legislation and oversight agencies.

This project saw the debut of a newly redesigned web and social media presence, but we also seized this opportunity to take a deep dive into all existing applications to improve the user experience across the board. At the end of the day more than 800,000 references to the Dept. of Mines, Minerals, and Energy were changed, 40+ applications were redesigned, a new website came to life, and the project was so successful that official guidelines were put in place to follow our processes to serve as guidance for agency name changes in the future.

Reenergize Virginia

A multi-year campaign that involved partnerships with state and federal agencies, municipalities, and private industry to promote renewable energy opportunities within Virginia. We created a series of standalone web applications, interactive dashboards, and videos to educate potential stakeholders on the Virginia Clean Economy Act. This campaign culminated in the largest new renewable energy project in the United States, seeing Siemens Gamesa and Dominion Energy parter to create hundreds of new jobs on a $200 million project in Portsmouth, VA.

Adventure Haven

Established a brand from the ground up for a family focused outdoor resort that offers one of a kind experiences while also seeking to bring a dated industry into the current era of technology.

Created a brand strategy that revolved around the family values, vision, and the mission of Adventure Haven. Concepted and designed logo, typography, photography, graphics, and slogan. Developed a unique marketing plan that combined traditional in-person marketing strategies to form meaningful connections with local businesses as well as utilizing online methodologies that would allow for developing buyer personas and tracking conversion rates. All information was consolidated into a business plan that was then used to pitch to investors to secure additional funding.
